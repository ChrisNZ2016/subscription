## Summary

**Where we sit in the broader market:**

**2kg bags:** Our $27.50/kg (RRP) sits above ACANA ($27.00/kg RRP, $22.95/kg Pet Perks) and squarely alongside Hill's ($30.55/kg RRP, $25.97/kg Pet Perks) and Pro Plan ($28.00/kg RRP, $22.40/kg on sale). It's competitive at RRP level, but once competitors' member and subscription discounts are applied, the effective price gap widens — ACANA Pet Perks drops to $22.95/kg, and its Autodeliver to $21.60/kg.

**6–8kg bags:** Our $21.00/kg (6kg) and $18.50/kg (8kg) position above ACANA's Pet Perks rate ($17.28/kg at 6kg) but below Orijen at comparable sizes. Our 8kg at $18.50/kg is competitive with Orijen's 11.4kg Pet Perks rate ($20.21/kg) — a strong signal that we're in super-premium territory.

**Subscription pressure:** Autodeliver/Repeat Delivery discounts compress effective prices significantly at competitors. A Petdirect Autodeliver customer buying ACANA 2kg pays $21.60/kg first delivery — just $5.90/kg less than our standard retail. At 6kg sizes, ACANA autodeliver (ongoing $17.28/kg) leaves a wider gap versus our $21.00/kg retail ($16.80/kg at 20% subscriber). If we plan a subscription offering, that gap narrows our headroom.

**Tier positioning:**
- Budget/mainstream: Black Hawk Original ($8.81–14.50/kg Pet Perks)
- Mid-premium: Hill's, Pro Plan, Royal Canin, ACANA ($14.67–22.95/kg Pet Perks)
- Our product: $14.58–27.50/kg — top of mid-premium / entry of super-premium at standard retail
- Super-premium: Orijen ($20.21–35.27/kg Pet Perks)

**Key risks:**
- ACANA at 6kg ($17.28/kg Pet Perks) undercuts our 6kg ($21.00/kg) by ~$3.72/kg — significant for a comparable premium grain-free product
- Competitors' subscription pricing brings effective costs down further (ACANA Autodeliver ongoing $17.28/kg vs our 20% subscriber $16.80/kg)
- The 2kg entry price ($27.50/kg RRP) is defensible only if sensitive stomach positioning is clearly communicated
- Pet Central (Nutrience) at $28.63/kg for 2.27kg is a useful comparable — a single-price retailer with no loyalty discounts, sitting just above our 2kg price point
- Royal Canin 3kg RRP is now confirmed at **$78.49** ($26.16/kg) — previously estimated at ~$73.52. This is higher than estimated, meaning Royal Canin sits closer to our pricing tier than the original doc suggested
